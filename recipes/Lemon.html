{No Bake} Key Lime Cheesecake Parfaits

What is Key Lime Cheesecake Parfaits ?

These No Bake Key Lime Cheesecake Parfaits are a favorite around here â€“ with a cheesecake like filling, buttery graham cracker crumbs, sweetness from condensed milk, and tartness from key limes, they are amazing!

Recipe Overview

The components that makes up this recipe are the crust and the filling. After we make those, two we will assemble them.

Ingridients

  • 1 2/3 cups finely crushed Graham cracker crumbs (about 14 whole sheets)
  • 8 tablespoons unsalted butter , melted
  • 16 ounces cream cheese , softened
  • 3 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/3 cup key lime juice
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lime zest
  • Whipped cream

Steps

For the crust:

  1. Crush up the graham cracker sheets until you have crumbs.
    You can do this by placing them in a resealable plastic bag and using a rolling pin, or a food processor.
  2. Transfer the crumbs to a bowl and mix with the melted butter until moistened and combined.

For the filling:

  1. Using a handheld electric mixer, beat the cream cheese and powdered sugar together until smooth and creamy.
    Beat in the condensed milk, key lime juice, sugar, and lime zest until creamy and well incorporated.
  2. Place in the fridge for 1-2 hours to firm up. (This step is optional, but it makes it easier to pipe.)
  3. Transfer mixture to a piping bag.

Assembling

  1. Alternate the graham cracker crumbs and the cheesecake mousse into 8 ounce glasses (starting with the crackers and ending with the mousse.)
  2. Chill for a few hours to completely set.
  3. Finish with some piped whipped cream. Garnish with a slice of key lime or zest.
